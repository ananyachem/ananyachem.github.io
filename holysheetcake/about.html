ABOUT ME

welcome to my baking website!! i am ananya, a 20 year old engineering student with a passion for baking. creating this website is the best of both worlds for me, as i get to showcase my best recipes and the pictures i've taken along the way as well as build this website from scratch.


baking is my way of unwinding and enjoying myself. i usually jam to music while baking which just makes it so much more fun. baking is a therapeutic way to spend your time, plus you get amazingly good desserts in the end!


these are some of my tried and tested recipes, the ones which always yeild the best baked goods. this website has been curated using my favourite recipes by my favourite bakers.

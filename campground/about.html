ABOUT US!

Camp Ground is an amazing website curated for beginner, intermediate and advanced level campers. We offer different activities just to make your camping trip extra special. You could get the whole camping experience for as little as $50. Since breakfast is the most important meal of the day, we also provide a hearty complimentary breakfast everyday during your stay which is free of any charges!

BEGINNER LEVEL

If you're just looking for a weekend getaway, we've got you covered. This trip would be the perfect way to unwind and enjoy yourself after a stressful week of work. Get the full camping experience by staying in your own tent, attending campfires and hopefully spotting a wild animal or two. Pricing starts at $50 per person.


INTERMEDIATE LEVEL

This package is for the more adventurous people, who've got a little bit more time on their hands. It includes your full camping experience along with a special hike through the beatiful mountains surrounding the area. You will be accompanied by a tour guide with years worth of experience to ensure you have the best hike of your life. Pricing starts at $150 per person.


ADVANCED LEVEL

Our most exciting package of all! All these activities are done over the span of 10 days. This package includes a wonderful camping experience, along with a surreal hike through the mountains and a ravishing mountain climb later. Beginners will be given training before the mountain climb. You will be provided with all the gear needed for you climb. All you need is a few essentials and a determined mind to make this your best climb. Pricing starts at $200 per person.

CAMPING

Roasting marshmallows, watching the stars, experiencing an eerie night time silence, all while cozying up next to a warm campfire. If I were you, I'd never leave!

HIKING

If you're not too into fitness but still want to make the best of your trip, this hike is the perfect activity for you. It encases a small hike along the mountains, crossing a beautiful waterfall.

MOUNTAIN CLIMBING

If you're a litle more into fitness and love to break a sweat, get ready for this breathtaking (yes, literally) experience! Beginners can attempt to climb the smaller mountain nearby.
